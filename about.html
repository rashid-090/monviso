Which paint is best for living rooms?

For living rooms, we recommend our velvet or pearlescent finishes. They offer warmth, depth, and elegance while remaining easy to maintain and visually striking in both natural and artificial lighting.

Can your paints handle humid conditions well?

Yes! Many of our finishes, like lime coatings and concrete textures, are moisture-resistant—making them ideal for humid climates, especially in bathrooms, kitchens, or coastal locations.

Do you offer custom texture and colors?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom-blended textures and color palettes that suit your exact style, mood, and space, because no two walls, or visions, are the same.

Is Monviso paint easy to apply?

Most finishes require professional application for the best results. However, we provide clear guidance and work with skilled applicators to ensure smooth, consistent, and high-quality finishes every time.
